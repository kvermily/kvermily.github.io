"I declare after all there is no enjoyment like reading!"

-Pride and Prejudice

It is a truth universally acknowledged that a single reader, having recently finished a book, must be in want of another. But what to do when you've worked your way through Jane Austen's entire catalogue?

Austentatious was created to be your literary matchmaker to novels inspired by Jane Austen's classics. Select a Jane Austen novel and we'll pair you with your next great read.
