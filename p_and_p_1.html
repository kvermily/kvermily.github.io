Unmarriageable

Soniah Kamal

"It was a truth universally acknowledged, Alys suddenly thought with a smile, that people enter our lives in order to recommend reads."

This clever and cutting novel set in early 2000s Pakistan features heroine Alys Binat deconstructing gender, classism, and post-colonial identity in her charming repartee with Mr. Darsee.

Readers will also enjoy the appendix feating copious notes and resources and an exhaustive recommended reading list, Kamal's love letter to global literatures.
